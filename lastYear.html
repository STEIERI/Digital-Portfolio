About Me

I love messing with things, which has caused me to have tons of interests: animating, sketching, skateboarding, singing, gaming, and, of course, programming. I am studying to become a video game designer, due to my fascination of different types of media, I wanted to find a way to combine all of my hobbies together into one large passion. I started my programming "career" when my mom signed me up for Scratch when I was about four. Since then, I have been developing and mastering my skills, even applying non-coding related things to help me understand programming better.
