The ISP

After many attempts, we decided that our plan is to make a game on Unity with fast and engaging gameplay with fun aesthetics, made for people who want something satisfying to both play and watch.

Some roles include
  • Main programmer
  • Graphic designer
  • Social media manager
  • Treasurer

Competition is tough, so our unique combat system would provide something new to the audience, and hopefully bring in more consumers. We'd advertize it on social media to have people relate to us and our goal. We would sell the game on Steam where we would also get the money from them, and our only expenses would be the percentage of our revenue taken away by Steam in order to display it on their website and purchasing a Unity license which is required if we're to make money off of the project.
